The Fine Art Of Sandwich Making





The act of making a sandwich can be as simple or complicated as your appetite desires. While a peanut butter sandwich can be prepare in as little as a minute, an authentic Philly cheesesteak will require a bit more effort. A sandwich can be prepared multiple ways (grilled, open-face and even deep-fried) with countless varieties of breads, meats, cheeses, vegetables and condiments to choose from. Today, we will be making an American classic: the club sandwich.



Club Sandwich

  •   Prep time: 10 minutes
  •   Skill: Beginner


Ingredients:

  •   Bread (toasted)
  •   Ham
  •   Turkey
  •   Bacon (cooked)
  •   Cheese
  •   Lettuce (rinsed)
  •   Tomato (sliced)
  •   Mayonnaise


















Directions

  1. Place three slices of toasted bread on a cutting board or clean surface. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice.

  2. Layer turkey, bacon, cheese, lettuce and tomato on the first the slice of bread.

  3. Layer ham, bacon, cheese, lettuce and tomato on the second the slice of bread.

  4. Place one of the topped slices of bread on top of the other toppped slice.

  5. Place the untopped slice of bread last, mayo side down.

  6. Cut the sandwich into two or four sections. Devour!

    7.   Tip: To ensure the layers are kept intact, place a toothpick in the middle of each section, prior to cutting.
